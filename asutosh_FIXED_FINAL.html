About

Asutosh Kashyap is a distinguished cinematographer and filmmaker with a decade of experience, known for evocative storytelling and masterful control of light and composition.

His directorial debut Síbuk — The Untamed Journey was prominently featured at the 18th Mumbai International Film Festival (MIFF 2024). In 2021, he was honoured with the Best Cinematographer award for Sijou at the Canadian Cinematography Awards. His work has earned recognition from National Geographic's YourShot as an Editor's Favourite.

Beyond filmmaking, Asutosh is a Canon Mentor, TEDx Speaker, and a Generative AI Filmmaker — currently writing and composing stories for Wishtales AI, exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and narrative artistry.

He leads Utpaat Vision and Creations, a creative production house dedicated to cinematic excellence.

Sijou Film Still

2021
Documentary Film · Cinematography

"Sijou"

The poignant story of a young boy in the village of Saikhonguri, whose life drastically shifts as he becomes a victim of the Feudal Land Tenure System in Bhutan. Directed by Vishal Chaliha, Asutosh's cinematography brought this vibrant narrative to life — earning the Best Cinematography Award and a place in the Indian Panorama section at IFFI 52.

🏆 Canadian Cinematography Awards · Winner

Síbuk Film Still

2024
Documentary Film · Director & DOP

"Síbuk — The Untamed Journey"

A compelling documentary offering a heartfelt exploration of the lives and challenges faced by indigenous communities in Northeast India. Asutosh's dual role as director and cinematographer showcased his profound storytelling abilities — earning nominations for Best Documentary and Best Debut Director at MIFF 2024.
